WHY VOTE FOR US?

Our mission at Project Blue is to ensure that all students earn the grade that they deserve. We understand students cannot always be perfect one hundred percent of the time and we believe that every student deserves a second chance to improve their learning.

We understand students are busy and may not always be able to commit the time they feel is necessary to perfect a project and their understanding of the material. Through Project Blue, students will be able to correct a project which they feel they did not perform their best.

Through our platform, students will be able to remediate their scores and enhance their comprehension of history concepts as well as feel more confident in their abilities. Vote G Period for a brighter future!
